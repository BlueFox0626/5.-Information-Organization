Program Highlights

Our Designer Track will teach you how to research, design, and build the frontend and UI for modern web applications. Bloc programs are intensive and outcome-oriented. If you're looking for an immersive experience with the flexibility of being online, this program includes:

  • 1-on-1 Mentorship With an Experienced Designer
  • Learning By Building Real Applications

Who Should Take Our Track?

This program is for beginners. No previous design experience is required. But be prepared for a rigorous, challenging program that requires consistency, discipline, and accountability. By the end of this program, you will have the skills to switch careers and become a professional designer.

Check out our UX/UI or Frontend Course if you are looking to enhance your career rather than launch a new one.

Master Every Part of the Design Process

In the Designer Track, you'll start with design fundamentals and UX/UI design where you'll learn the essence of problem solving through conducting research, creating wireframes, and designing engaging web and mobile applications. After that, you'll dive deeper into HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and jQuery - the fundamentals of frontend programming. You'll leverage your design skills to create stunning websites and assets, and implement them with you frontend programming skills.

1 Phase I

UX/UI Foundation Foundation

Learn Design Fundamentals

Learn the fundamentals of design and apply your newfound knowledge to projects to showcase in your portfolio.

Learn the UX/UI Design Process

Explore the UX/UI design process from start to finish as you conduct user research, create wireframes, and design the user interface for BlocBox.

Design Mobile & Emerging Tech Apps

Learn mobile design as you create apps for the iOS and Android platforms, and explore emerging technology platforms.
