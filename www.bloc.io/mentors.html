Our mentors are experienced.

Bloc mentors have an average of over 9 years of experience in their field.

Our mentors give back to the technical community.

Bobbilee organizes Rails Camp USA West Coast

Bobbilee continues her passion for mentoring by organizing and leading Rails Camp USA West Coast, a yearly non-traditional unconference for unplugging and expanding knowledge in the development and design community.

Eliot gives back to the open source community

Eliot contributes to popular repositories, including Rails and Ember. He also makes screencasts to help teach experienced frontend developers from all over the world under Web Dev Break.

Brittany helps local students at Railsbridge in Pittsburgh

And passes on the passion for teaching to her students. Alumni Jake Wengroff says, “I finally got to meet Brittany Martin! I was in Pittsburgh last weekend for her Railsbridge event, where I served as a TA."

Leading the way in mentorship.

Levi Kennedy, Lead Mentor
