Program Highlights

Our UX & UI Design Fundamentals teaches the basics and industry standard tools of design. You’ll conduct user research, develop wireframes, design visual mockups, and create prototypes for usability testing. Bloc programs are intensive and outcome-oriented. They combine an immersive learning experience with online flexibility:

  • 1-on-1 Mentorship With an Experienced Designer
  • Real Web & Mobile Application Design

Who Should Take Our Course?

For anyone looking to learn about UX & UI Design. Beginners and seasoned designers alike welcome.

UX & UI Fundamentals Syllabus

Foundation

In Bloc’s UX & UI Design Fundamentals, you’ll become a problem solver. You’ll focus on user-centered design and apply research methods to isolate user goals. You’ll learn to create personas, user flows, wireframes, and visual mockups using tools like Balsamiq, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Sketch. You’ll perform usability tests using interactive prototypes, and become comfortable with presenting, selling, defending, and critiquing your work.

Develop user personas and user flows after conducting research
Learn design tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Sketch, Balsamiq, and InVision
Master wireframing, visual design, and usability testing

Projects

In the Project Phase of your apprenticeship, you’ll leverage the fundamentals learned in the Foundation Phase as you work on a series of open-ended projects. You’ll showcase your designs in a custom portfolio to demonstrate your talent. During the Project Phase, your mentor will be your client, senior designer, and project manager. Your mentor will provide project requirements, feedback, design critiques, and advice.
